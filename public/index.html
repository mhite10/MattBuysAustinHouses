Frequently Asked Questions

We do our best to make the process of selling your house as transparent as possible. Below are some questions frequently asked by our clients.

What is the cost for your services?

The costs of our services are completely free to you. There are no hidden fees, closing costs, or real estate agent commissions. We earn from purchasing the property, fixing it up, and renting or selling for a profit. In the event that we do not have sufficient bandwidth to purchase the property ourselves, we will allow a colleague the opportunity to purchase the property for a small assignment (finder’s) fee.

What if my property is underwater or in foreclosure?

We purchase properties that are underwater or in foreclosure. We utilize several unconventional methods for purchasing properties, which allows us to make an offer on a house that is worth far less than what is owed to the bank. Likewise, we are able to make offers on properties that are in foreclosure, provided that the process has not yet been finalized.

I don't live near my property, do I need to meet in person?

The entire sales process may be completed remotely and does not require you to travel or meet in person. We will coordinate the property walkthrough with the current tenants and submit an offer via e-mail or fax. You will be required to complete your side of the closing at a local attorney’s office and your money will either be deposited into your account or mailed to your home address.

Can you still purchase my house if it's currently listed with a real estate agent?

We make offers on houses currently listed with real estate agents. However, depending upon on your agreement with the real estate agent you may still be required to pay the agent’s commission fees.
